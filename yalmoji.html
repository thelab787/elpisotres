Story

Yalmoji is an emoji mobile application for iOS and Android. The application introduces a puerto rican character in funny situations and provides puerto rican slang and phrases to share. The application is inspired by the puerto rican culture, its music and celebrities.

The Application provides over 200 yalmojis to share and is divided in 3 categories: emojis, stickers and messages.

To share an emoji simply choose the emoji you want to share, press the share button and then select the platform on which you want to share. Emojis can be shared through all popular messaging platforms and social networks: SMS, Whatsapp, Facebook, Messenger, iMessage, Instagram, Twitter, Email etc.

The application also provides an emoji keyboard that allows you to share emojis without having to open the application. New stickers are added constantly, without the need to update the application.

Yalmoji is available for free to download in the App Store (iOS) and the Play Store (Android). The application is written entirely in Spanish and requires a minimum version of iOS 10.2 for iPhone / iPad or Android 4.1.
